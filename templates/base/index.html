How SmartPikin Works

Transforming Education in Sierra Leone through AI and Innovation

  • Centralized Educational Portal

    Search, filter, and view profiles of schools, tutors, and educational resources in one place. Empower students and guardians with easy navigation and real-time updates.

  • AI-Powered Tools for Educators

    Create innovative lesson plans, generate math exercises, summarize documents, and get creative writing assistance tailored for Sierra Leone's needs.

  • Empowering Students & Guardians

    Easily discover schools, track applications, and find tutors, all through an intuitive, AI-driven platform designed for Sierra Leone's unique challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the SmartPikin project and how it’s transforming education in Sierra Leone.

SmartPikin is an AI-powered educational platform designed to address Sierra Leone’s educational challenges. It connects students, guardians, schools, teachers, and tutors while offering tools like lesson plan generators, creative writing assistance, and application tracking.

Educators can use SmartPikin to create modern, tailored lesson plans for rural and urban settings. It also provides tools for generating math exercises, summarizing documents, and fostering creativity in classrooms.

Students and guardians can discover schools and tutors, track applications, and access educational resources. Teachers can enhance their lesson plans, and schools can better engage with the educational community.
